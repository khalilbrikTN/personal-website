FAQ

What is your typical turnaround time?

It depends on project scope. A landing page takes 3–7 days. A full web app or AI integration typically takes 2–6 weeks. I'll give you a clear timeline in the proposal.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes — all projects include revision rounds. I work iteratively and won't close a project until you're satisfied with the result.

What information do you need to get started?

A brief description of what you want to build, your target audience, any design references you like, and your approximate timeline and budget.

Can you work with an existing codebase?

Absolutely. I'm comfortable joining ongoing projects, doing code reviews, or extending existing systems regardless of the original stack.
