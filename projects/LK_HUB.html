The Challenge: Breaking SaaS Dependency

Liberating Knowledge required more than a website—they needed a proprietary operational engine. The organization’s mission was being hampered by fragmented, high-cost SaaS tools. The objective: Build a unified system capable of managing five independent divisions (Education to Policy) from a single, high-security command center. Total cost of ownership: Zero.
