From Cairo to D.C.: 104 Years of Diplomatic Ties

January 2026 Diplomacy Mohamed Khalil Brik

I had the privilege of attending “From Cairo to D.C.: 104 Years of Egypt–U.S. Diplomatic & Economic Ties: Building Bridges, Forging Pathways & Unlocking Potentials,” a timely and insightful discussion featuring Christopher Landau, U.S. Deputy Secretary of State.

The conversation highlighted how diplomacy, education, and economic cooperation continue to shape the future of Egypt–U.S. relations, and the role young leaders and institutions play in strengthening these ties.

Grateful for the opportunity to engage with distinguished academics, professionals, and fellow students, and to be part of a dialogue centered on cross-cultural understanding and collaboration.

Proud to represent the Tomorrow's Leaders Program community in spaces where global conversations turn into real pathways.
