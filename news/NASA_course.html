Why Titan?

Most intriguing destination in our solar system with unique characteristics for potential life discovery.

  • Dense Atmosphere: Only moon with substantial atmosphere
  • Hydrocarbon Cycle: Methane rain, rivers, and lakes
  • Organic Chemistry: Complex prebiotic molecules
  • Subsurface Ocean: Liquid water beneath surface
  • Dynamic Weather: Seasonal changes and patterns

Primary Mission Objectives

Five key scientific goals designed to advance understanding of Titan's potential for harboring life.

  • Atmospheric Analysis: Nitrogen-methane atmosphere study
  • Surface Mapping: High-resolution geological imaging
  • Hydrocarbon Lakes: Methane/ethane lake investigation
  • Subsurface Ocean: Evidence detection for water ocean
  • Prebiotic Chemistry: Organic chemistry assessment
