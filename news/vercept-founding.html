Co-founding Vercept

November 2024 Mohamed Khalil Brik

I'm thrilled to announce an exciting new chapter in my journey - I'm co-founding Vercept, a revolutionary technology company that's set to transform the way we approach artificial intelligence and data science.

The Vision Behind Vercept

Vercept was born from a simple yet powerful idea: to bridge the gap between cutting-edge AI research and real-world applications. Throughout my academic journey at The American University in Cairo and my research collaborations with NASA, I've witnessed firsthand the incredible potential of AI technologies that often remain confined to academic papers.

Our mission at Vercept is to democratize access to advanced AI solutions, making them accessible to businesses and organizations of all sizes. We're building tools that don't just impress in research labs, but actually solve real problems in the real world.

What We're Building

At Vercept, we're developing a suite of AI-powered tools that focus on:

  • Computer Vision Solutions: Leveraging my experience with machine learning and image processing
  • Data Analytics Platforms: Making complex data analysis accessible to non-technical users
  • Educational AI Tools: Bridging my passion for education with cutting-edge technology

The Journey Ahead

This venture represents the culmination of years of learning, research, and preparation. From my early programming courses to my current work on advanced machine learning projects, every step has led to this moment.

I'm incredibly grateful for the support of my co-founders, mentors, and the amazing community at AUC that has shaped my technical and entrepreneurial mindset. The journey ahead is challenging, but I couldn't be more excited about the problems we're going to solve and the impact we're going to make.
