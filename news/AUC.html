Joining AUC with Tomorrow's Leaders Scholarship

September 2022 Mohamed Khalil Brik

I'm thrilled to announce that I have been selected among 5 Tunisians to be part of the Tomorrow's Leaders Program, a prestigious program sponsored by the US Department of State through the Middle East Partnership Initiative (MEPI). This life-changing opportunity will allow me to pursue my bachelor's degree at The American University in Cairo with a fully funded scholarship!

About the Tomorrow's Leaders Program

The Tomorrow's Leaders Program is a highly competitive scholarship initiative sponsored by the US Department of State through the Middle East Partnership Initiative (MEPI). This program identifies and supports exceptional students from across the Middle East and North Africa region who demonstrate academic excellence, leadership potential, and a commitment to making positive change in their communities.

Being selected as one of only 5 Tunisian recipients from hundreds of applicants across the country is both humbling and incredibly motivating. This scholarship represents not just financial support, but recognition of my potential to contribute meaningfully to my field and society.

The American University in Cairo

The American University in Cairo (AUC) is widely regarded as one of the most prestigious universities in the Middle East and North Africa region. Founded in 1919, AUC has established itself as a leading institution for higher education, combining American liberal arts education with the rich cultural heritage of Egypt and the Arab world.

Why AUC?

  • Academic Excellence: Ranked among the top universities in the MENA region
  • International Recognition: Accredited by both Egyptian and US educational authorities
  • Diverse Community: Students from over 50 countries creating a truly global environment
  • Research Opportunities: State-of-the-art facilities and cutting-edge research programs
  • Industry Connections: Strong partnerships with leading companies and organizations
  • Cultural Bridge: Unique position bridging American and Middle Eastern educational traditions

My Academic Journey Ahead

At AUC, I'll be pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, building upon the strong mathematical and scientific foundation I developed during my Baccalaureate studies in Tunisia. The program offers:

  • Comprehensive Curriculum: Covering algorithms, data structures, software engineering, and AI
  • Research Opportunities: Access to cutting-edge research in machine learning and data science
  • Industry Partnerships: Internship and collaboration opportunities with leading tech companies
  • Innovation Hub: Access to entrepreneurship programs and startup incubators
  • Global Network: Connections with alumni working at top tech companies worldwide
