About the College of Charleston

The College of Charleston, founded in 1770, is a prestigious public liberal arts university located in the historic city of Charleston, South Carolina. Known for its beautiful campus, rigorous academics, and strong research programs, the college provides an ideal environment for interdisciplinary learning and collaboration.

The opportunity to study abroad at such a renowned institution has allowed me to experience American higher education firsthand while engaging with cutting-edge research in computer science, data science, and space exploration.
