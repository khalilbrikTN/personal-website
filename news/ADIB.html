About Abu Dhabi Islamic Bank - Egypt

Abu Dhabi Islamic Bank (ADIB) Egypt is an award-winning financial institution that began operations in Egypt after acquiring the National Bank for Development (NBD) in 2007. As part of a consortium between Abu Dhabi Islamic Bank and Emirates International Investment Company (EIIC), ADIB Egypt has established itself as the largest Islamic bank in Egypt.

With over 2,000 employees serving customers through a nationwide network of 70 branches and 40 microfinance units, ADIB Egypt represents a leading example of modern Islamic banking. The bank's commitment to providing Shari'a compliant banking solutions while maintaining cutting-edge technological infrastructure makes it an ideal environment for learning about cybersecurity in the financial sector.
