The AI Company: A New Beginning

October 2025 The AI Company Mohamed Khalil Brik

My journey in AI began five years ago when I started learning Python and traditional machine learning in high school. I challenged myself through Kaggle projects, including one in which I predicted COVID-19 outcomes based on the health procedures countries were implementing. That project revealed how deeply interconnected data, policy, and real-world impact truly are.

When ChatGPT emerged, it marked the beginning of a new direction in AI. It sparked my curiosity about how large language models are actually built, leading me to dive deep into LLM architecture and training. At university, I continued this path by designing an AI system for emergency response as part of my research at AUC. I later presented this work in the US, where I deepened my understanding of building reliable, failure-tolerant AI systems and reducing model hallucinations.

During those years, I also studied software architecture. Initially, I thought it only applied to traditional systems; however, I came to realize that the same principles of reliability, modularity, and clear system boundaries are exactly what make AI systems work at scale. That mindset shapes how I design AI today.

Now it's time to put these skills to work solving real business problems that impact millions of people.

At The AI Company, we believe real innovation comes from strong architecture: finding smart, practical system designs that solve hard problems while staying aligned with solid ethical standards.

Beyond building solutions, we want to create a tech community, a space for people who are curious and passionate about AI to connect, learn, and grow together. Throughout this journey, we'll share what we learn, the challenges we face, and the approaches we take to deliver our solutions.

This is just the beginning. Stay tuned. We'll be announcing our first project very soon.

Mohamed Khalil Brik
