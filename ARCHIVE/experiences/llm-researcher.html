LLM Researcher

The American University in Cairo Aug 2024 - Dec 2024 Cairo, Egypt

Project Overview

Led research on "Using Large Language Models to Enhance Emergency Response Systems: A Context Awareness Approach". The project aimed to revolutionize 911 systems by integrating AI to support human dispatchers.

Key Responsibilities

  • Developed LLM system to analyze 911 call texts and classify emergencies.
  • Integrated age recognition from voice inputs to tailor LLM recommendations.
  • Drafted comprehensive operational guidelines manual ensuring alignment with best practices.

Achievements

  • Presented research at the National Conference on Undergraduate Research (NCUR) 2025 in Pittsburgh, USA.
  • Engaged with over 60 attendees during a dedicated two-hour poster session.
  • Achieved 76.5% accuracy in Address Extraction and 48.3% in Name Recognition.

Technologies Used

  • LLMs: LLaMA 2, GPT-4
  • NLP: BERT-large-cased for NER
  • Speech-to-Text: OpenAI Whisper
  • Frameworks: Python, PyTorch, Hugging Face
